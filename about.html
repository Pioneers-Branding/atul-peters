About the Surgeon

Transforming Lives Through Advanced Surgery

Prof. (Dr.) Atul N. C Peters is a luminary in the field of surgery, whose illustrious career spans over a quarter of a century. As Vice Chairman of the Department of Bariatric, Minimal Access & Robotic Surgery at Max Smart Super Specialty Hospital, Saket, he leads one of India's highest-volume bariatric programs.

Dr. Peters has been awarded as CENTRE OF EXCELLENCE for Bariatric and Metabolic Surgery by OSSI effective from 01.01.2020 and also got awarded as CENTRE OF EXCELLENCE for Robotic and Innovative Surgery by ARIS effective from 02.03.2024. Dr. Peters is also a 'Proctor' in Robotic surgery and provides real-time guidance, feedback and support to a trainee surgeon during a robotic procedure.

He is a DHA Certified, Specialist Bariatric and Robotic Surgeon at New Modern Hospital, Dubai and consultant Bariatric Surgeon at NMC, Muscat. With close to three decades of surgical experience, his practice is now dedicated to advance GI, minimal access and robotic surgery with special interest in Bariatric & Metabolic Surgery for diabetes, performing over 1500 cases annually.
