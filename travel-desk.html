After confirmation of a mutually agreed date, we would request you to transfer 50% of the surgery fees as an advance deposit which will help us confirm the surgery slot for you on that date. The remaining funds can be paid on the day of admission before the surgery.

We would arrange for a complimentary transfer between the airport and the hospital. At the Airport, our travel associate from the hospital/clinic will receive you with a placard with your name on it.

We operate from three different hospitals; the usual airport to hospital traveling time is 30 minutes but may be more at times due to traffic conditions.

When you reach the hospital, you will be escorted to the international patient lounge and one of our staff will help you with the admission process.
