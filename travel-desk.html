How It Works

Your Medical Journey

A streamlined, 4-step process designed to prioritize your health, comfort, and peace of mind.

01
Initial Phase

Query & Virtual Consultation

Send your medical reports to our team. Dr. Peters will review them and conduct a detailed online video consultation to discuss concerns and formulate a personalized treatment plan.

02
Preparation Phase

Visa Invite & Travel Booking

Upon transferring a 50% advance deposit to confirm your surgery date, we will immediately issue the necessary Medical e-Visa Invitation Letter for your embassy appointment.

03
Arrival Phase

Airport Pickup & Admission

Enjoy a complimentary VIP airport transfer. Our associate will greet you with a placard, escort you to the International Lounge at the hospital, and expedite your admission process.

04
Treatment Phase

Surgery, Recovery & Follow-up

Your attendant stays with you during admission with complimentary meals. Post-discharge, we assist with hotel accommodation and provide long-term digital health support after you fly home.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Comprehensive logistics and medical answers for international patients

Travel How do I reach the hospital from the airport?

We arrange a complimentary airport transfer between Indira Gandhi International Airport (DEL) and the hospital or your hotel. A dedicated travel associate will receive you at the arrival terminal with a placard displaying your name and escort you to a private vehicle.

Travel How far is the hospital from the airport?

Our hospital facilities are located in Saket, New Delhi. Under standard traffic conditions, the travel time from Indira Gandhi International Airport is approximately 30 to 45 minutes.

Travel What is the admission process upon arrival?

Once you reach the hospital, you will be escorted to our exclusive International Patient Lounge. Our dedicated coordinators will manage all admission paperwork, registration, and coordinate directly with clinical staff to ensure a seamless experience.

Travel Can my companion stay with me in the hospital room?

Yes. One attendant or companion is permitted to stay with you in your private room throughout your hospital admission. We provide them with a companion bed and complimentary daily meals.

Travel Can you help organize local sightseeing tours?

Absolutely. We partner with reputable local travel providers to arrange custom sightseeing tours across Delhi, Jaipur, or the Taj Mahal in Agra. Chauffeur-driven private vehicles can also be booked upon request.

Travel When is it safe to fly back home after surgery?

Most patients are medically cleared to fly back home **7 to 10 days after surgery**. This timeline allows us to perform a thorough post-operative evaluation and ensures that you are walking comfortably, hydrated, and fully past the early recovery phase. We will issue a formal medical clearance certificate for the airline prior to your travel.

Safety Is weight loss surgery safe for international travelers?

Yes. When performed in our center of excellence using advanced minimally invasive (laparoscopic or robotic) techniques, the safety profile is extremely high (complication rates below 1%). We take special clinical precautions for international travelers, including pre-flight DVT prevention strategies and custom travel clearance checks.

Safety Will I experience severe pain after the procedure?

Pain is typically mild and highly manageable because we perform these operations through small keyhole incisions (laparoscopy). We use advanced post-operative pain protocols, allowing most patients to walk comfortably within hours of their surgery.

Recovery How long is the hospital stay?

The standard hospital stay is 2 to 3 nights. This allows our clinical team to monitor your initial recovery, manage pain, and guide you through the initial liquid hydration phase before discharge.

Recovery What kind of diet will I follow after surgery?

Your diet progresses in clear phases: clear liquids for the first 7 days, pureed protein shakes in week 2, soft foods in weeks 3-4, and small portions of solid foods starting in week 5. Before you leave, our specialized dieticians will sit down with you to establish a personalized nutritional plan.
