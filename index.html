Generally, you may be a candidate if you have BMI above 35, or BMI above 30 with obesity-related conditions like Type 2 diabetes, high blood pressure, or sleep apnea. The best way to determine eligibility is to schedule a consultation with Dr. Peters.

Yes, bariatric surgery is one of the safest elective procedures when performed by an experienced team. With over 25 years of experience and 15,000+ surgeries, Dr. Peters maintains a complication rate well below the national average using advanced laparoscopic techniques.

Metabolic surgery is highly effective for Type 2 diabetes. Studies show 80-90% of patients achieve diabetes remission or significant improvement. Procedures like gastric bypass resolve diabetes by improving insulin sensitivity, often within days to weeks after surgery.

Most patients stay in hospital for 2-3 days after laparoscopic surgery. Light activities can resume within a week, and most people return to work within 2-4 weeks. Full recovery typically takes 4-6 weeks with structured diet and follow-up care.

Many insurance plans cover bariatric surgery, especially with obesity-related health conditions. Our team assists with insurance verification and provides necessary documentation. We also offer flexible payment plans for self-pay patients.

We provide lifelong follow-up care including regular check-ups, nutrition planning, support groups, and 24/7 emergency contact. Our team ensures you have continuous support throughout your transformation journey.
