About Peter's Fitness Factory

Medically Supervised Rehabilitation

Post-bariatric exercise requires a specialised clinical approach — protecting healing abdominal tissue, preventing joint strain, and targeting visceral fat while building lean muscle.

Joint-Protective Motion

Our orthopedic recumbent cardio trainers and guided pin-loaded resistance machines ensure zero joint impact on knees, lower back, and hips during your weight loss journey.

Preserving BMR & Muscle

Rapid weight loss can cause muscle loss if unguided. Progressive strength conditioning keeps your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) elevated so you burn fat continuously while preventing loose skin.

InBody 770 Telemetry

Regular clinical bio-impedance body composition scans track your precise ratio of visceral fat loss versus skeletal muscle gain throughout your bariatric recovery milestones.

Clinical Progression

The 3-Phase Rehab Protocol

Our bariatric trainers collaborate directly with Dr. Atul Peters and your nutritionist to guide you through a medically structured exercise progression.

Phase I — Mobility & Cardio

Focusing on gentle circulation enhancement, diaphragmatic breathing, and low-impact recumbent cycling without putting stress on abdominal core sutures.

Weeks 2 to 6 Post-Op

Phase II — Progressive Strength

Introducing biomechanically guided pin-loaded resistance machines and light dumbbells to tone muscle and protect BMR as your weight drops rapidly.

Months 2 to 6 Post-Op

Phase III — Metabolic Mastery

Achieving peak physical vitality, full-body functional strength, and permanent weight maintenance with customized athletic conditioning routines.

Months 6+ & Lifelong
