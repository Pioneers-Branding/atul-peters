Advanced Bariatric Surgery, Close to Home

Dr. Atul Peters is a senior bariatric and robotic surgeon with 25+ years of surgical experience and more than 5,000 successful bariatric procedures to his credit. Patients from Preet Vihar, Mayur Vihar, Vasundhara, Indirapuram, Kaushambi, Sahibabad, Shahdara, Vivek Vihar, Krishna Nagar, Geeta Colony, Laxmi Nagar, Nirman Vihar and Yamuna Vihar regularly travel to his practice at Max Smart Super Speciality Hospital, Saket for weight loss surgery and metabolic (diabetes) surgery.

Bariatric surgery is not a cosmetic procedure — it is a life-saving, evidence-based treatment for obesity and its associated diseases. Depending on the type of procedure, patients typically lose 60–80% of their excess body weight within 12–18 months, with significant improvement or complete remission of:

  • Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus (70–90% remission)
  • Hypertension & high cholesterol
  • Obstructive Sleep Apnoea (OSA)
  • Joint pain & osteoarthritis
  • PCOS & infertility in women
  • Non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD)

Despite being Delhi's leading bariatric centre, Max Smart Saket is only a short drive from most East Delhi neighbourhoods. The hospital offers cashless insurance, modern ICU facilities, and a dedicated bariatric nursing team — making it a convenient and safe choice for your weight loss journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions from East Delhi Patients

Which is the best bariatric surgery hospital for East Delhi patients?

Max Smart Super Speciality Hospital, Saket is the preferred choice for most East Delhi patients. It is a JCI- and NABH-accredited multi-specialty hospital with a dedicated bariatric unit led by Dr. Atul Peters, modern ICU facilities, and cashless insurance tie-ups. The commute from most East Delhi neighbourhoods is 20–30 minutes via the Ring Road or DND Flyway.

How long does it take to reach Max Smart, Saket from East Delhi?

Most East Delhi locations are within 20–30 minutes of Max Smart, Saket by road, depending on traffic. From Preet Vihar, Mayur Vihar, Laxmi Nagar and Shahdara, the typical drive is under 25 minutes. Patients from Indirapuram, Vasundhara and Sahibabad can also use the DND Flyway for a smoother commute.

Is bariatric surgery covered under insurance?

Yes. Most health insurance policies now cover bariatric surgery for patients meeting the clinical criteria (typically BMI ≥ 35, or BMI ≥ 32.5 with comorbidities). Max Smart Hospital offers cashless hospitalisation for all major insurance providers and TPAs, including CGHS, ECHS and PSU schemes. Our care team will help you with pre-authorisation.

What is the cost of bariatric surgery in Delhi?

The cost depends on the type of procedure, hospital stay, and insurance coverage. As a reference, bariatric surgery in Delhi typically ranges from ₹2.5L to ₹5.5L. Robotic and revision procedures may be higher. EMI options are available for self-pay patients. Exact pricing is shared after your consultation.

Do I need a referral to consult Dr. Atul Peters?

No referral is required. You can directly book a consultation at Max Smart, Saket. Our care team will help you schedule an appointment, share documents to bring, and answer any preliminary questions about your case.

How soon can I return to work after surgery?

Most patients return to light desk work within 5–7 days after a laparoscopic or robotic sleeve gastrectomy or gastric bypass. Heavy physical activity is typically resumed after 4–6 weeks. Robotic surgery often allows for an even faster recovery.
